Bloom Studio: A Minimal Webflow Template Built for Creative Agencies
Go to Project
Digital Studio
Bloom was designed and developed as a flexible Webflow template for creative agencies, studios, and freelancers who need a polished online presence without fighting against their own brand identity.

The design is intentionally minimal and neutral, built so it can adapt to different visual languages without clashing with existing branding.
The structure covers everything an agency typically needs: project showcases, team pages, service breakdowns, and contact flows, all built with clean CMS collections and reusable components so the site is easy to manage and scale over time.

Every interaction, layout, and responsive breakpoint was crafted from scratch in Webflow, with a focus on performance, clarity, and making the template feel like a custom build rather than a generic starter.
